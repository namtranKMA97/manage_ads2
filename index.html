Manage All Your Facebook Assets in One Place

Say goodbye to manual work, and hello to Ads Manager 2.0!
Manage My Ads
Get accurate performance data for all your assets in one view

Easily understand how your assets perform, filter them by asset type, funnel stage, ad spend, and your key metrics. This way, you can immediately identify low-performing assets and decrease their budgets or turn them off. Moreover, once you find well-performing assets, you can instantly scale their budgets.

Get Data
Make changes to MULTIPLE assets AT ONCE

On Madgicx’s Ads Manager 2.0, you can simply tick all the assets you’d like to optimize and apply changes to all of them in ONE CLICK. You can even decrease or increase all of their budgets by percentage, which saves you the manual work of adjusting each asset’s budget separately.

Manage Assets
Keep up with performance trends

It’s nice to know the average performance of your assets over a certain period of time, but that’s not enough. You must be able to tell whether your asset is CURRENTLY taking up or down. This is exactly why you need graphs that show your ROAS over time.

Stay on top of your automated ads

Automating your ads? Smart choice! In Ads Manager 2.0, you can view all your active ad automations on each of your assets to keep everything in order. This will take you one step further toward managing your ad account on auto-pilot.

Efficiently collaborate on the same account

Do you have a team that works together on the same Facebook ad account? Or maybe you work in a digital advertising agency and manage client accounts? On Ads Manager 2.0, you can view all the changes made to your assets and who made those changes. Additionally, you can learn how every change affected the asset’s performance.

Get immediate creative analysis

Over time, your ad performance will fluctuate. This happens to all Facebook advertisers. But you have to understand what causes these fluctuations, and for that, you need in-depth creative analysis. In Ads Manager 2.0, you can easily obtain an AI-based analysis of your selected assets, which includes images, videos, and ad copies.
